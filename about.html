The Story Behind Repz Your Style

Our Beginning

Repz Your Style was born in 2019 from a small but passionate team who lived and breathed street dance culture. What started as a simple dream — to unite dancers from around the world and celebrate their unique styles — quickly became a platform for creativity and community.

From the very start, we wanted Repz Your Style to be more than just another dance battle. That's why the first edition in 2019 included not only street dance battles but also a K-Pop dance cover competition, opening the stage to more dancers, more diversity, and more energy.

The Pause

Just as momentum began building, the world was hit by the COVID-19 pandemic. Like many others, we had to put everything on hold. No events, no gatherings, no chance to bring people together. It was a difficult time — but we never gave up on our vision.

The Comeback

In 2023, Repz Your Style 2023 made its long-awaited return — bigger, better, and more exciting than ever. With the theme “More Activities, More Experiences, More Fun,” we expanded beyond dance into a full cultural experience. That year, our event featured: Street dance battles & K-Pop dance covers Art exhibitions & creative workshops A vibrant local bazaar with over 80 handicraft & food vendors Repz Your Style 2023 became a milestone, made possible by the overwhelming support of dancers, artists, vendors, and our community.

Growing Stronger

Encouraged by the response, and with the dedication of our partners, we pushed forward into Repz Your Style 2024 and Repz Your Style 2025. Each year grew bigger than the last — with new categories, expanded line-ups, and the honor of welcoming more than XXX international teams and dancers to Malaysia.

From street dance to K-Pop, from art to community markets, Repz Your Style has become a festival of culture, connection, and creativity.

Our Vision

Repz Your Style is not just a competition — it's a movement. A space where people of all backgrounds can come together to dance, express, create, and connect. We believe in celebrating diversity, empowering young talent, and building bridges across cultures through the universal language of movement and art.

And this is only the beginning.
